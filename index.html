Julián Medrano Hoyos | Interdisciplinary Reasearcher

I’m an interdisciplinary researcher specialized on the intersections between philosophy and art. Since 2021 I have been exploring the philosophical and cultural implications of emerging technologies like bitcoin while building a solid foundation in blockchain.

Alongside my research, I collaborate with the Fundación Archosauria, a non-profit organization in Colombia that manages Vivarium del Caribe, a nature reserve for fauna and flora of the Caribbean.
